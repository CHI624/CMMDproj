CMMD

CMMD is a Machine Learning Model Project that utilizes multimodal data to generate a comprehensive classification of the input data, seperated typically between three classes, Complementary, Similar and Unrelated.

Current Model

Army Personnel typically go into a military situation with many unknwown variables, these variables can range anywhere from unforseen enviormental factors, hidden enemy contacts and a lack of knowledge on the enemies intentions.

CMMD RISK CHOICE SIMULATOR

Corrections to Current Model

Usually after going through a military situation, regardless of mission success or failure, army personnel, have to fill out associated paperwork in relation to the mission based on a set of predefined rubrics given to us by the military, but these rubrics are either too vague or non specific to grasp the severity of a situation.

So to leverage our current Machine Learning Model, we beleive that by redefining these set standards to clearly define risk factors, we can not only accurately classify specific situations, but integrate machine learning models to help us understand mission data and make specified recommendations based on the key inputs given to us by military personnel.
