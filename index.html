ARMOR



Adaptive Reasoning for Mitigation of Operational Risk (ARMOR) is a reasoning module for identifying, assessing and providing recommendations for hazards.

Current Challenge

Army Personnel typically go into a military situation with many unknwown variables, these variables can range anywhere from unforseen enviormental factors, hidden obstacles and limited knowledge on the adversary intentions.

Current Approach



Currently Army Personnel uses standard reports and documents to perform multiple steps in the risk management process.

ARMOR Approach



To leverage reasoning modules to assist in the steps within the risk management process.
